## ✅ Hostkey

- Host key is used for...
- 1️⃣ maximizing windows
- 2️⃣ controlling use of mouse and keyboard
- 3️⃣ shortcuts

## ✅ Two main shortcuts

- shortcuts are used when VM interface does not have buttons, very useful
- `Host` would be `Control Key on the Right`(you can custom it)
- `Host + F`: maximizing the VM screen, make VM full screen
- `Host + E`: to take a screenshot
- `Host + T`: to take a snapshot, recovery point
